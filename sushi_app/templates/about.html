About our Establishment

Established in 2020 by a group of fresh college graduates, we are a restaurant that aims for the highest level of customer satisfaction in the Chicagoland area. We can guarantee you that by eating at our restaurant, you will get to enjoy the freshest fish possible.

Please reach out to a manager with any birthday inquiries.
